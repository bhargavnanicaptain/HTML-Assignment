ECLATECS 2K23

ECLATECS 2K23: ECLATECS 2K23 was a National level technical symposium organized by the department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at JNTUA College of Engineering Kalikiri.

Decennial Celebrations: The symposium was part of the decennial celebrations of JNTUA College of Engineering Kalikiri, held on 10th March 2023.

Coordinator: Dr. K. Aparna served as the coordinator for ECLATECS 2K23, leading the event with dedication and expertise.

Committees: All faculty members and non-teaching staff were actively involved as members in various committees, contributing to the successful organization of the symposium.

Participation: More than 100 students from various colleges actively participated in different events organized by the ECLATECS 2K23 team.

Events: The symposium featured a variety of technical events designed to engage and challenge participants, showcasing their skills and knowledge in electronics and communication engineering.

Networking: ECLATECS 2K23 provided a valuable platform for students to network with peers, faculty, and industry professionals, fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing.

Recognition: The event recognized and rewarded outstanding participants, encouraging excellence and innovation in the field of electronics and communication engineering.

TEACHERS DAY BY ECE STUDENTS

2019-2020 Teacher’s Day Celebrations: Teacher is defined as a ‘Motivational Candle‘ of a student's life. Teachers play a major role in making their students responsible citizens of tomorrow and good human beings.

Importance of Teachers: It is impossible to imagine our lives without teachers. To respect them, especially apart from studies, we celebrate Teachers' Day.

Event Date: On 5th September, the students of the ECE Department took the opportunity to show their gratitude towards their teachers.

Event Location: Students celebrated this event in the Seminar Hall of the ECE department by garlanding Sri Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan garu.

Gratitude: They paid their warm thanks and respect to their teachers by giving speeches.

Program Leadership: This program was led by final year ECE students.
